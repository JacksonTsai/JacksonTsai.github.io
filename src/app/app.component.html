What is Progressive Web App

Progressive Web Apps are websites. Look and feel like an app, users will browse Progressive Web Apps on their browser with an URL just like they do any website, but right after they land on the PWA, they get the experience of using an “app”, right on their browser, without the need to download and install.

Reliable

Load instantly and never show the downasaur, even in uncertain network conditions.

Fast

Respond quickly to user interactions with silky smooth animations and no janky scrolling.

Engaging

Feel like a natural app on the device, with an immersive user experience.
